## What we build

### Custom internal systems (CRMs, ERPs, portals)
For companies managing clients, orders, or teams in Excel or a generic CRM that doesn't match their process. The exact tool they need - no monthly licences, no adapting the business to a template.

### MVPs in 6 weeks
Booking platforms, marketplaces, B2B tools, internal management apps. We ship just enough to validate with real users - first customers are testing the product while competitors are still in Figma.

### Process automation
We connect stores, ERPs, CRMs, and accounting systems. Orders flow into the ERP automatically, invoices generate themselves, reports run without human intervention. Measurable ROI in 2-3 months.

### Legacy system modernisation
We migrate from Access, monolithic PHP, or Excel to modern architectures. While we build the new system, the old one keeps running. End users notice nothing - the business notices everything.

## How we work

Most agencies share one thing: the senior who sold the project disappears once it starts.

At Doscientos, the engineers on the call are the ones who build the product.

**6-week process:**

| Week | What happens |
|---|---|
| 1 - Discovery | 60-min call to lock scope, not accumulate questions |
| 2 - Proposal | Deliverables, timeline and fixed price before writing a line |
| 3-4 - Development | Working demo every Friday, fast iteration, no bureaucracy |
| 5 - Testing | Tests with real users, bugs fixed in 24-48h |
| 6 - Launch | Production deploy, analytics, and 30 days of support included |

**Commercial terms:**

| | Doscientos |
|---|---|
| Price | Fixed, agreed before we start |
| Payments | 30% upfront / 40% midway / 30% on delivery |
| Code ownership | 100% client's |
| Communication | Weekly demo + Slack throughout |
| Post-launch support | 30 days included |
| Urgent changes | Response within 24-48h |
