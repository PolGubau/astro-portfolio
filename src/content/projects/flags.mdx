# React Beauty Flags

**React Beauty Flags** is a React library that provides over **250 handcrafted SVG flags** ‚Äî from countries to cities ‚Äî designed to bring color and style to modern interfaces.

Born from frustration with dull or over-complicated alternatives, it focuses on **simplicity, visual harmony, and developer experience**.

## Motivation

After reusing flags in dozens of projects, I realized existing solutions were either too heavy or lacked design consistency. Inspired by **Duolingo‚Äôs clean and playful visuals**, I decided to design a set that looked great in any modern UI while staying developer-friendly.

> ‚ÄúI wanted something elegant enough for portfolios but simple enough to drop into production.‚Äù

The result is a **library now downloaded over 1,000 times** (and growing!) through NPM, with strong adoption among UI-focused developers and open-source enthusiasts.

---

## Highlights

* **+250 Handmade Flags** ‚Äî Countries, cities, and symbols designed in Figma.
* **Lightweight & Fast** ‚Äî Zero external dependencies, optimized SVGs.
* **Customizable** ‚Äî Supports Tailwind utilities for borders, shadows, and radius.
* **Search & Filter** ‚Äî Built-in search experience with sharable URLs.
* **TypeScript Ready** ‚Äî Fully typed, autocompletion in your IDE.
